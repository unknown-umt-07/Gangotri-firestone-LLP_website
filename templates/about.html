About the Company

Leading the Way in Construction Materials

We would like to introduce ourselves as a premier manufacturer of a wide range of high-quality construction chemicals. Originally Gujarat-based and known as one of the best manufacturers and suppliers of AAC Block Joining Mortar in India, we sell our products under the brand name HEXOCHEMS.

GANGOTRI FIRESTONE LLP is a mid-cap adhesive manufacturer with state-of-the-art production capabilities located in Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra. We offer flexible, innovative solutions for the most challenging construction and waterproofing applications.
